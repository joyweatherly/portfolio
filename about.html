About Me

Hello! My name is Joy Weatherly and I was born and raised in Georgia. Growing up, I have always been more artistic than not. In class I was always doodling and I loved every art class that I took growing up.

However, when I went off to college, I chose to go into a Psychology major at the University of Georgia as I felt that I would have a more secure job in that area. I quickly grew tired of it and started thinking about changing my major. At this time I had also built my first PC and wanted to start working with computers in some way.

After my Sophmore year, I switched to a Computer Science major but quickly dicovered that was not for me when I started failing classes for the first time in my life.

Because I was failing, I panicked and dropped out of school for a semester while I thought about what I wanted to do with my life. I really wanted to incorporate art and design into my major and combine those two with a bit of CS.

This lead to me applying and getting accepted to Kennesaw State University and changing my major to Interaction Design. I have been loving my new major and created this website as part of my classes in this major.
